Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the monthly fee?

The monthly fee includes hosting, security updates, technical support, and basic maintenance to keep your website running smoothly.

How long does it take to build my website?

Starter websites are delivered in 3 business days. Business and Pro websites typically take 5-7 business days depending on complexity.

Can I upgrade my package later?

Yes! You can upgrade to a higher package at any time. We'll simply adjust your monthly fee accordingly.

Do you offer custom packages?

Absolutely! If your needs don't fit our standard packages, contact us for a custom quote tailored to your specific requirements.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept EFT, credit cards, and PayPal. Setup fees are paid upfront, and monthly fees are billed automatically.

Is there a contract?

We work on a month-to-month basis. You can cancel anytime with 30 days' notice. No long-term contracts required.
